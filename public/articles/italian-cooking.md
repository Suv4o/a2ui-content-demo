## The Perfect Pasta Water

> "Pasta water should taste like the sea" â€” Every Italian Grandmother

Here's the golden rule:

1. Use a **large pot** - pasta needs room to move
2. Add **plenty of salt** - about 1 tbsp per liter
3. **Never add oil** - it prevents sauce from sticking
4. **Save some pasta water** - the starch helps bind sauces

## A Simple Recipe: Aglio e Olio

This 5-ingredient masterpiece proves that simplicity is sophisticated.

**Ingredients:**

-   400g spaghetti
-   6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
-   1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
-   1 tsp red pepper flakes
-   Fresh parsley, chopped

**Method:**

1. Cook pasta until al dente
2. Slowly cook garlic in olive oil until golden
3. Add pepper flakes, remove from heat
4. Toss with pasta and a splash of pasta water
5. Finish with parsley and serve immediately

## Regional Diversity

Italy's 20 regions each have distinct culinary traditions:

-   **North** - Butter, cream, risotto, polenta
-   **Central** - Wild game, truffles, hearty stews
-   **South** - Tomatoes, seafood, spicy flavors
-   **Islands** - Fresh fish, couscous, unique traditions
